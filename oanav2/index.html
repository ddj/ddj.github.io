About ME

Hello

I'm Oana, a product designer based in London.

I am a creative problem solver whose superpowers are imagination, empathy and mastery of the design thinking process. My experience spans all phases of the design lifecycle, from carrying out user research and analysis to brainstorming and prototyping solutions, in the form of wireframes and high fidelity screens. Beyond digital products, I've also enjoyed projects involving service design and organisational strategy.

I like to be inquisitive, I throw myself into my work and don't shy away from a tough challenge. I bring an enthusiastic approach and a "can-do" attitude to the table and I'm at my happiest in a room full of energized people, sharing ideas.

My mission is to innovate while making a positive impact.
