Hey! I'm David Johnson, specialist in User Experience & Product Design,
based in London, UK.

I'm a Digital Designer who firmly believes that UI/UX design is NOT the 'cherry on top'. I love to create visual flare and 'wow!' moments, as expectations of digital products have rapidly risen. The combination of User Experience and User Interface is now the first impression for a user, and we all know just how much first impressions count!

Designing is my passion, but given my engineering background I also enjoy prototype development. I believe having the knowledge of how a product is implemented is important for a well-rounded and effective designer. I aim to create product designs that are not only user friendly and visually stunning, but also developer friendly.

If a picture is worth a thousand words, then a prototype is worth a thousand meetings.
