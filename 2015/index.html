ABOUT ME

I'm a Digital Designer and Engineer who firmly believes that UI/UX design is NOT the 'cherry on top'. Rather it is an integral piece to the modern puzzle of success, where the quality and expectations of digital products have rapidly risen and still continue to grow.


The combination of User Experience and User Interface is now the first impression for a user, and we all know just how much first impressions count!


Designing is my passion, but given my engineering background I delve in the building process as well. I like to keep up with the latest dev tools and trends as I believe having the knowledge of how a product is implemented is important for a well-rounded and effective designer. I aim to create designs that are not only highly usable and visually stunning, but also developer friendly, as if the implementation is too complex, you've only created a concept.

PRODUCT DESIGN PROCESS



To give an insight into how I work I've outlined the different stages I go through during a project. It's also worth noting that occasionally some of these steps are skipped or merge toegther depending on the scope and timeframe for the project.
Discovery Analysis

A lack of business knowledge and incorrect assumptions can nullify the best of designs. It's important for me to gain a summary view and understanding of key business elements such as USP, product philosophy and competition etc.

User Research & IA

For end users to benefit from a tailored design process matching their exact needs, the correct identification of target audience groups is crucial. This will be achieved through persona creation, storyboarding, and red route analysis.

UX Design

Sketches and wireframes visually represent the user reearch and product requirements. These high level, abstract diagrams push usability to the forefront, looking objectively at feature placement, ease of use, conversion paths and more.

UI & Visual Design

Once the user flow has been solidified, moodboards, colour palettes, and styling will be carefully and creatively constructured to attract target users and portray the philosophy of your brand, whilst maintaining a consistent look and feel.

Interaction Design & Prototyping

Static images alone are not enough to create intuitive experiences. Defining state changes and micro-interactions will bring the product to life, through iterative prototyping which ensures the product behaves as the user expects it would.
